ABOUT

HI, I’m Dinny Ren, raised in Adelaide and currently based in Melbourne. I am a graphic designer, 3D artist, storyteller, and explorer of emotions. I create graphic works, 3D art, UX/UI, fine-line drawings and indulge in the unrealistic world. I am deeply intrigued by Nihilism, spirits and the relation between humans and other living organisms and how they influence one another as the world develops. Through interactive 3D environments and conceptual digital projects, I aim to tap into the colours of feeling, memory and imagination. I believe that understanding and reflecting on the past, present, and future of human actions can facilitate more meaningful connections with the spiritual side of us Thank you for viewing my world from a third perspective. Please don’t hesitate to reach out for any exciting ollaborations, I’m always ready to create something beautiful together :)
