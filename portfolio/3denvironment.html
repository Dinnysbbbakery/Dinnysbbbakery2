VOIDSPACE

The world voidspace, is an abstract, post-apocalyptic space, capturing vibrant, dreamcore, surrealistic environments. The world is inhabited by floating organisms gory and creepy in nature. They have no feelings and their goal is to eat each other to survive. The world is a portrayal of the overall purposelessness of life, that the world ends and everyone dies so everything we do has no meaning.

After Effects, Premiere Pro&Procreate

WELCOME

The project ‘Welcome’ is an interactive environment that takes place in both realistic and fantasy worlds, guiding the player through the cycle of birth, life, death, and rebirth. Drawing on the cultural symbolism of different regions, I incorporated elements like butterflies, mushrooms, and other objects to represent concepts such as death, the soul, and dreams.

Unity&Blender
