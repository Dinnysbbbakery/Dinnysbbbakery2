Handheld Paper Windmill

Materials List

  • One sheet of foam
  • Scissors
  • Glue Dots
  • Ruler
  • Pen
  • Straw
  • Pipe Cleaner

Cut the foam sheet into a 12.5cm square. Fold the foam in half into a triangle in both directions to make an ‘X’ then punch a hole in the cross-section.

Cut 4 more cuts halfway along the fold lines then punch 4 more holes 1 on every angle of the square. Apply tape around the 4 holes then match them to the centre hole.

Cut 2 small foam rings.

Wrap the pipe cleaner around one end of the straw, leaving 5cm hanging. Slide the foam ring, windmill and foam ring one by one onto the pipe cleaner in order, push them down. Cut the excess pipe cleaner off and wrap the rest like a snail to secure everything.
